About

I am an avid programmer who enjoys learning new things about programming. I am most experienced in Javascript and Python, but I also have experience with C#, HTML/CSS, SASS and SQL.

I received an Advanced Diploma in Electro-Mechanical Engineering (Robotics & automation) from Humber College in Toronto, Ontario. I learned how to program PLCs, six-axis robot, and Integrated circuits. After graduating, I built bots, desktop apps, and focused on back-end development before setting my sights on a Full-Stack development career.
